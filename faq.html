Frequently Asked Questions

📅 Dates & Registration
When are the 2026 Robocamp sessions?
Three sessions in 2026:

🟦 Elementary Session 1: June 1–4, 2026
🟣 Middle School (6th–8th): June 15–18, 2026
🟩 Elementary Session 2: July 13–16, 2026

All sessions run Monday–Thursday, 9:00 am – 5:00 pm.
How do I register my child?
Register at westwoodrobots.org/robocamp and pay at rrisdpay.com → High Schools → Westwood → Robotics Club. Spots are first-come, first-served! A 3% processing fee applies.
What is the payment deadline?
Payment deadline is Monday, June 1st. Register early — spots fill up fast! Join the waitlist if a session is full.
💰 Cost & Lunch
How much does Robocamp cost?
💰 $325 — without lunch (bring your own)
🍕 $350 — with daily Domino's Pizza lunch

A 3% processing fee is added. Snacks are included for ALL campers!
Is lunch provided?
If you choose the $350 option, Domino's Pizza is provided daily for lunch. With the $325 option, campers bring their own lunch. Either way, morning and afternoon snacks are provided by the camp!
🎒 What to Bring
What should my child bring each day?
✅ Reusable water bottle
✅ Extra snacks
✅ Lunch (if not opting for pizza)
✅ Closed-toed shoes (REQUIRED — no sandals!)
✅ Sunscreen (pre-applied + extra)
✅ Backpack
✅ Swim clothes for Thursday Splash Day 💦
✅ Positive attitude! 😊

Camp provides: safety goggles, computers, activity materials, and snacks!
Does my child need to wear specific shoes?
Yes — closed-toed shoes are required every day! No sandals, flip-flops, or open-toed shoes in the Makerspace. Plan accordingly!
Is there a water/Splash Day?
Yes! 💦 Thursday is Splash Day for both elementary and middle school programs. Pack swim clothes for your child to change into. Water activities happen in the morning — it's a fan favorite!
🏥 Health & Medical
Is there a nurse on campus?
No — there is no nurse on campus. Camp staff cannot administer medication. If your child needs medicine during the day, a parent or guardian must come to campus to give it. Please disclose all allergies and medical conditions during registration.
Can staff give my child their medication?
No — staff will NOT administer any medication (including EpiPens, inhalers, or daily meds) except in a life-threatening emergency. A parent or guardian must come to campus to administer medication.
My child has a food allergy — what do I do?
Disclose all allergies in the registration form. Camp staff use this info to take appropriate precautions. For specific concerns, email westwood_robotics@roundrockisd.org before camp starts.
📋 Rules & Policies
What happens if my child misbehaves?
Consequences escalate:

1️⃣ Temporary removal and separation from the group
2️⃣ Sent home for the day (parents notified, school admin notified)
3️⃣ Suspended from camp for the week — no refund issued

For illegal acts, police may be notified.
Will my child use power tools?
No — all power tools are completely off-limits! Heavy machinery is taped off. Robot-building uses "no cut" methods only. Safety goggles are provided when needed.
Will photos of my child be published?
Yes — by participating, parents agree to allow photos to be published and used by Westwood Robotics (social media, website, etc.). This is included in the registration waiver.
Can the schedule change?
Yes — the schedule can change at any time without notice. The Robocamp team are problem-solvers who will adapt as needed to keep camp safe and fun! 🛠️
📍 Location & Drop-off
Where is camp held?
📍 Westwood High School — Makerspace, Room C1307
12400 Mellow Meadow Dr, Austin, TX 78750

Drop-off and pick-up at the Main Entrance (blue circle on campus map).
What time is drop-off and pick-up?
🌅 Drop-off: 9:00 am · 🌆 Pick-up: 5:00 pm

Both at the Main Entrance. Please be on time for pick-up — counselors are available until ~5:15 pm.
