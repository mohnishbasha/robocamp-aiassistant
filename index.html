🎯 What Campers Do

30+ Hands-On Activities!

Every single day is packed with engineering, science, coding, creativity, and tons of fun. No boring lectures — just doing!

🤖

Robot Building

Build FLL LEGO robots or FTC competition robots from scratch using real parts.

💻

Coding

Block coding for elementary, Java for middle school. Real programming skills!

🔬

Science Experiments

Elephant Toothpaste, Oobleck, Lemon Batteries, Rain Clouds, and more!

🖨️

3D Printing & Laser

Design in TinkerCAD → 3D print it. Make a custom pendant on the Glowforge!

🌉

Engineering Challenges

Build a Bridge, Egg Drop, Paper Airplanes, Penny Boats — real engineering design!

🎮

Drive Real Robots!

Take the controls of FTC and massive FRC competition robots. So cool!

🍦

Science Snacks

Homemade ice cream with science + s'mores baked in a solar pizza oven!

💦

Splash Day! 🎉

Thursday is Splash Day — outdoor water fun. Pack swim clothes!

❓ Questions

Common Parent Questions

Quick answers to things parents ask most. Our AI assistant can answer anything else!

Is there a refund if my child can't attend?
If a camper is suspended for rule violations, no refund is issued. For other cancellations, contact us at westwood_robotics@roundrockisd.org as soon as possible.
Is there a nurse on campus?
No nurse on campus. Staff will not administer medication. If your child needs medicine during the day, a parent must come to campus to give it. Disclose all allergies during registration.
Will my child be photographed?
By participating, parents agree to allow photos to be published and used by Westwood Robotics. This is included in the registration waiver.
Can the schedule change?
Yes — the schedule can change at any time without notice. The Robocamp team adapts as needed to keep camp safe and fun for everyone!
