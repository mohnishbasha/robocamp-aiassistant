Sessions & Registration

Three sessions across the summer — choose what works for your family! All run Monday–Thursday, 9 am – 5 pm.

🟦 Elementary — Session 1

June 1 – 4, 2026

Monday through Thursday  ·  9:00 am – 5:00 pm

Starting from
$325
👧 Who
Elementary School
Grades K–5
🤖 Program
FLL Robots + Science
30-min rotation activities
📍 Location
Westwood High School
Makerspace C1307, Austin TX

Young engineers rotate through hands-on science and STEM activities, build LEGO robots (FLL), explore 3D printing, and take home a laser-cut pendant. The week ends with a Battle Bots competition and Splash Day! 🎉

🤖 FLL Robot Build 🔬 Science Experiments 🖨️ 3D Printing 💻 Coding 🔹 Laser Cutter 💦 Splash Day

🟣 Middle School

June 15 – 18, 2026

Monday through Thursday  ·  9:00 am – 5:00 pm

Starting from
$325
👦 Who
Middle School
Grades 6–8
🤖 Program
FTC Robots + Java Code
Competition-level robotics
📍 Location
Westwood High School
Makerspace C1307, Austin TX

Middle schoolers build real FTC robots with REV hardware, program them in Java, drive on a full competition field, and learn CAD design in Onshape. Counselors explain how real FTC & FRC competitions work — opening doors to joining robotics teams this fall! 🚀

🤖 FTC Robot Build ☕ Java Programming 📐 CAD Design 🎮 Drive FTC Robots 🔴 FRC Demo 💦 Splash Day
