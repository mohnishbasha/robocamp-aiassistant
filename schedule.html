🗓 What Happens Each Day

Daily Schedule

A full day of robotics, science, coding, and fun from 9am to 5pm. Here's the play-by-play!

⚠️

The schedule is subject to change at any time without notice. Our team adapts to keep camp safe and fun!

⏰ At a Glance

A Typical Camp Day

Every session follows a similar rhythm packed with variety — never a boring moment!

🕘 Daily Timeline
9:00 – 9:30
Check-in & Warm-Up 👋
Name games, Legos, card games, Go Noodle
9:30 – 11:00
🔬 Rotation Activities 3 × 30 min
Three hands-on stations — science, engineering, robotics
11:00 – 11:20
🍎 Snack Break
Break outside the Makerspace (no snacks inside!)
11:20 – 12:20
🛠️ Main Activity
Science experiments, engineering challenges, or group builds
12:20 – 1:00
🍕 Lunch
Domino's Pizza (if opted in) or bring-your-own
1:00 – 2:15
⚙️ STEM Challenge
Bridge building, Egg Drop, TinkerCAD, or robot coding
2:15 – 3:00
⚽ Recreation — Dodgeball!
High-energy outdoor fun every afternoon
3:00 – 3:30
🎬 Snack + Chill
Movie time or robotics presentation with snacks
3:30 – 4:45
🤖 Robot Build & Code
FLL or FTC robot building and programming sessions
4:45 – 5:15
🏠 Check-Out Time
Board games, coloring, winding down. Parent pick-up at 5:00 pm at Main Entrance.
